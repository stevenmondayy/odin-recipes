Orange Chicken and Steak Meal

A newer creation, using a sugar free orange glaze, this one is to die for!

What you'll need:

  • Two Chicken Bites
  • Two Steak Bites
  • Frozen Broccoli
  • G Hughes Orange marinade
  • Stovetop pan for heating
  • Foodsafe bowls for distributing

And now for the cooking instructions:

  1. Grab your stovetop pan, and set the heat to medium.
  2. Start heating your frozen broccoli in the microwave, according to package instructins. (While the pan is heating)
  3. After 5 minutes (about when the vegetables are done), oil up your pan, and add your chicken and steak.
  4. Heat up the chicken and steak, season to taste if desired.
  5. Drop the heat to low so you don't overcook the steak bites, and pour over 90 grams of the orange marinade!
  6. Grab your foodsafe bowls, and evenly split the heated vegetables.
  7. After 4 minutes on heat, evenly distribute the meats as well.
  8. Enjoy your meal, and now you also have an equal sized meal for later!

I sometimes trade the Orange marinade for straight Teriyaki marinade, for a different flavor profile.

This one is a great way to shake up the BBQ Chicken and Steak meal.
